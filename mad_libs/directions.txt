Mad Libs is the world’s greatest word game—and can make anyone the funniest person in the room! 
Mad Libs are stories with words removed and replaced by blank spaces.  
When prompted, fill in the blanks with adjectives, nouns, exclamations, colors, adjectives, and more. 
These words will be inserted into the blanks of the story for hilarious results.  
There are no winners or losers, only laughter. 
Have fun with it and let's begin!
